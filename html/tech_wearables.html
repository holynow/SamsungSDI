Battery of Things ! Battery for IT & Wearables

In line with diversifying IT trends, including notebook computers, smartphones, and wireless earphones, batteries are becoming smaller. Small batteries are used not only for IT devices but also for power tools, electric bikes and other products. Samsung SDI has developed differentiated IT batteries in accordance with diversifying needs.

IT devices and parts have been developing at a fast pace, from laptop computers to notebook computers and from feature phones to smartphones as well as tablet PCs that have become necessities in our daily lives, wireless earphones and smartwatches. In line with diversifying IT trends, batteries have become smaller. Samsung SDI has worked on new materials and innovative manufacturing process to realize higher volumetric energy density, keeping up with changing customer demands.

Small batteries were used for the conventional wired devices, allowing us to overcome physical constraints. Devices that are used to be powered by fossil fuel have been replaced with battery-powered devices. Today, small batteries are at the center of the battery market, reflecting greater interest in green technology. With the advent of the Battery of Things (BoT), where everything is powered by batteries, small batteries will be used for various applications, including power tools, e-bikes and robots.

In response, Samsung SDI has increased the energy density of pouch cells for IT devices to extend the usage time and raised the capacity of cylindrical batteries to use them for various types of e-mobility, including e-bikes.

In addition, development of small cylindrical batteries and coin cells designed for wireless earphones is in progress. In particular, the demand for coin cells has risen by a large margin, highlighting Samsung SDI’s technological competence in the IT sectors.

Samsung SDI has realized extremely high energy density in small batteries by adopting an innovative design in very small space and achieved ultra-fast charging technology through continuous research on materials and structure.

These innovative small batteries are widely used for Samsung Electronics IT devices such as Galaxy Buds Live and Galaxy Watch. Please keep an eye on innovation driven by Samsung SDI’s small batteries that are highly recognized in the smartphone and wireless earphone markets.
