All-in-one ESS, Samsung Battery Box(SBB)

Samsung SDI has recently developed SBB (Samsung Battery Box), putting separate ESS batteries into one box. SBB features higher safety and performance as well as advanced fire control system. Supplying containers with batteries together allows our customers to save costs and time significantly. Now, let’s check out Samsung SDI’s SBB, demonstrating its effort to value its customers’ voices.

At Interbattery 2022, Samsung SDI’s SBB (Samsung Battery Box), a new ESS solution drew huge attention. Separate battery cells, modules and other parts required for ESS are put together in a box-shaped SBB.

To begin with, energy storage system (ESS) refers to a device to store energy in order to supply the energy at a later time when needed. That way, ESS enables effective energy consumption and management as well as energy cost saving.

However, a series of fires broke out at ESS facilities. Worse, there are cases where it was difficult to identify the causes. Market participants were deeply concerned as the battery industry had suggested conflicting opinions whether a defect in product itself or a mistake in installation and operation was the cause.

To prevent fires in ESS, Samsung SDI has made SBB minimizing safety and performance degradation as well as fire risks that users may have encountered during installation and operation.

Samsung SDI supplies the pre-assembled ESS containing battery cells and modules and completes the installation in a so-called ‘Drop-Down’ way. SBB is highly recognized as a solution with advanced safety and convenience as customers can immediately use the ESS, simply by connecting the ESS with the grid.

Samsung SDI is expected to grow its presence in the ESS market by providing optimized ESS solutions based on customers’ needs.
