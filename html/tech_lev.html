Welcome to new e-mobility, Battery for Light Electric Vehicle (LEV)

Light electric vehicles (LEVs) are varying from low voltage to high voltage system, including kick scooters, bikes and scooters as the means of short trips as well as autonomous tractors, golf carts and commercial vacuums. Samsung SDI has worked on developing small cylindrical battery for LEVs, which are to be the means of green transportation in the future.

The Fourth Industrial Revolution has led many industrial sites to become smarter, including smart farms and smart factories. Innovative light electric vehicles (LEVs) are on the surface accordingly. For instance, autonomous tractors perform agricultural operations without human intervention and battery-powered ships are replacing the conventional ships, which account for 3 % of the global GHG emissions. The demand for zero-emission LEVs is rapidly growing as the international community come to take actions to achieve carbon neutrality.

The applications of LEVs are expanding, ranging from low voltage system for e-bicycles, e-bikes, electric kick scooters or lawn mowers, commercial vacuums and golf carts to high voltage system for off-road vehicles, mini shuttles and mini trucks.

Electric kick scooters and e-scooters are expanding fast in the mid-and long-term, in line with growing preferences for private transport due to Covid-19 and introduction of e-mobility policies.

In response, Samsung SDI has conducted R&D on cylindrical small batteries and module packs, which are used for LEVs in general. To provide a complete LEV solution to meet customers’ needs, Samsung SDI will continue to improve LEV performance by developing customized cells, high voltage and low voltage BMS as well as cell modules and composite modules.

21700 50E consisting of NCA V2 and SCN V2 was the only solution for Samsung SDI up until 2020. But, given the fast-growing LEV market, Samsung SDI decided to provide high capacity 21700 53G, consisting of SCN V3, starting from 2022.

Additionally, Samsung SDI plans to launch 50GB, a new cell for higher safety in 2022, following 50E.

100W coil tests conducted by Samsung SDI have verified that 21700 50GB ensures advanced heat dissipation and mitigates heat propagation risks significantly. In other words, Samsung SDI’s LEV batteries guarantee both high capacity energy storage and safety.
