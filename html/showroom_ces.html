Showcasing Samsung SDI’s New Battery Brand, ‘PRiMX’ in the U.S.!

Professional Cho Yeong-won, Marketing Team (AEB)

Providing the maximum experience with
prime battery to customers

‘PRiMX’, the new battery brand with Samsung SDI’s identity of ‘Providing the maximum experience with prime battery to customers’ was introduced in the U.S.

It was right at the ‘CES 2022’, the global exhibition where we can see the latest technological trends from IT, home appliances to cars.

This year, Samsung SDI booth was located at Wynn hotel, the center of Las Vegas.

At the entrance, SDI welcomed customers with the giant PRiMX presentation stand and LED screen that plays promotion video clips. Going inside, all of SDI batteries from EV, ESS to small IT/Power batteries that have been changed into new brands were presented.

At this exhibition, we introduced various content regarding Core Technology that would provide customers with the maximum experience, in line with the brand message of ‘Prime Battery for Maximum Experience’. And we presented the best products including EV Gen.6 that integrates Samsung SDI’s latest technology, SBB Battery Enclosure for ESS, cylindrical cell 45T for power tools and others.

Advancing the future, ‘Beyond the everyday’

Meanwhile, the official event of CES 2022 was held at the Las Vegas Convention and World Trade Center (LVCC) under the theme of ‘Beyond the everyday’.

LVCC consists of three exhibition halls, the West Hall, the Central Hall and the South Hall. The three halls are connected via Tesla’s underground tunnels so that visitors riding in Model X and Y can easily move to other hall.

Looking at many robotic and AI companies that participated the exhibition, it felt like things that have happened only in sci-fi films are just around the corner.
