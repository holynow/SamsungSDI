Highlights of InterBattery 2022
PRiMX to create brighter futures

InterBattery 2022, South Korea’s largest battery exhibition, marks the 10th anniversary this year. The exhibition was held for three days from March 17th to 19th at the Convention and Exhibition Center (COEX).

For those who could not attend the exhibition, this post is ready. Let’s take a look at Samsung SDI booth!

The image of Samsung SDI’s boot

*InterBattery 2022 Overview

  • Date: Mar. 17(THU) ~ Mar. 19(SAT), 2022
  • Venue: COEX Hall A
  • Host: Korea Ministry of Trade, Industry & Energy
  • Organizers: Korea Battery Industry Association, COEX

Prime battery
for Maximum Experience, 'PRiMX'

Samsung SDI’s booth was the most eye-catching with the biggest booth size at the exhibition. This year’s concept was ‘PRiMX, Prime Battery for Maximum Experience’. The booth was mainly colored in black with the letter ‘PRiMX’ printed around the walls, making the booth more future-oriented and sleek.

And, the brand video on the mega-sized Curbed LED screen at the center grabbed the viewers’ attention.

Exhibition zone for PRiMX, SDI’s battery brand

Mega-sized Curved LED playing our brand video

Various applications powered by PRiMX,
ranging from EVs to AI robots

In the xEV Zone, the iX and the i4, BMW’s electric models, equipped with Samsung SDI’s Gen.5 battery, were displayed, honoring the Samsung SDI’s booth.

The BMW i4 and iX displayed at the booth

In addition, Gen.6 to be launched next year was presented at the exhibition. As the featured battery enables a vehicle to drive up to 700km on a single charge, viewers showed much interest on Gen.6

A visitor listening to the explanation about Gen.6

In the IT/Power Zone, Samsung SDI’s diverse form factors and applications, including the latest model ‘Galaxy S22’, IT devices, vacuum cleaners, and power tools were displayed.

The displayed IT devices and power tools

In the ESS (Energy Storage System) Zone, all line-ups were displayed, including utility/commercial and residential ESS as well as UPS.

Many showed great interest to Samsung SDI’s battery during the tour after the opening ceremony

Samsung SDI’s AI robot equipped with our cylindrical battery was also displayed. And, in the experience center designed a restaurant, you could see the demonstration of AI robot serving dishes as if it were in the restaurant. In particular, cat-like Bellabot stole people’s attention with its cute face.

BellaBot

'Brighter Futures with ‘PRiMX',
the story of Samsung SDI’s batteries and ESG

There was a special session, ‘Battery Talk’, where MC Sang-han Go helped viewers to learn about PRiMX brand with fun.

In the session, we also introduced Samsung SDI’s ESG activities to make green batteries as well as brighter futures driven by PRiMX. Specifically, Samsung SDI is working on ‘Ban on DSM’ to protect marine ecosystem, ‘RMI’ to address child labor and other ESG activities. Moreover, Yes or No quiz with the winning prize of ‘Galaxy Buds 2’ was prepared and many played the quiz with enthusiasm.

A winner got the prize

Master PRiMX!
Booth map event and Photo zone

Diverse events were prepared under the theme of PRiMX, including the booth map event to complete the booth map after visiting all zones and a photo zone with PRiMX printed on. Various PRiMX goods added more fun to viewers.

Photo Spot with PRiMX printed on

Viewers writing supporting messages

On the other hand, at ‘The Battery Conference 2022’ which was held along with the exhibition, Executive Vice President Nae-hyuck Chang, Head of AEB) Development Office, made a speech about ‘The Strategy for the Safe ESS’. He introduced SDI’s fire control devices to ensure higher safety in ESS.

EVP Naehyuck Chang speaking about the safety of ESS at ‘The Battery Conference 2022’

EVP Naehyuck Chang said Samsung SDI added insulation sheets between cells in ESS to prevent heat propagation and directly inject agent in a cylinder to the event cell in case of fire. He also stressed the need for setting up advanced ESS policy and changing the current electricity pricing system.

How was our story at the InterBattery 2022? It was a meaningful opportunity to showcase our new battery brand, PRiMX, and envision brighter futures with it. Thank you for reading.
